Indie App Spotlight pitch: a habit app built like a trading card game
```

Body:

```
Hi Michael,

I built CardHabit, an iOS daily challenge app that deals you five habit cards each morning, built on trading-card-game mechanics. You commit to the cards you can hold that day, and friends can play Challenge cards into your day, like Raise the Bar or The Tempter. Four archetypes (Ironclad, Sage, Alchemist, Phantom) fit different styles of discipline.

The angle: it pushes back on the 75 Hard model. Hard things every day, not extreme things once.

## 2. Product Hunt draft

**Tagline (52 chars):**

```
A daily challenge that deals your habits as cards
```

**Description:**

```
CardHabit is an iOS daily challenge app that deals you five habit cards each morning, built on trading-card-game mechanics. You commit to the cards you can hold. Friends play Challenge cards into your day. The habit is the byproduct.
```

**Maker first comment:**

```
I made CardHabit after watching people around me quit 75 Hard and conclude they lacked discipline. The math was the problem, not them. Fixed extreme rules ignore that days differ. So CardHabit deals five habit cards each morning and you commit to what you can hold. Completed cards are immune. Friends can play Challenge cards into your day, which turned out to be the feature people talk about. It is free on iOS with a premium tier. I would love to hear what card mechanics you would want in a daily challenge.
```
